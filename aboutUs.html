About Us

Information About Us

Welcome to Coffee Shop, where every cup tells a story! Nestled in the heart of Armenia, we are passionate about bringing you the finest coffee experience. Our journey began with a simple love for coffee and a desire to create a welcoming space for our community. At Coffee Shop, we source our beans from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring that each sip is not only delicious but also supports ethical practices. Our expert baristas are dedicated to crafting the perfect brew, whether you’re in the mood for a rich espresso, a frothy cappuccino, or a refreshing cold brew. But we’re more than just coffee. Our cozy atmosphere invites you to relax, connect, and enjoy. Whether you're catching up with friends, diving into a good book, or getting some work done, we have the perfect corner for you. Join us for seasonal specials, local pastries, and community events that celebrate the art of coffee and the spirit of togetherness. At Coffee Shop, we believe that every cup is an opportunity to create memories. We can’t wait to share them with you
